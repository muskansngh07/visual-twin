2. basal_diameter - Refers to trunk's cross sectional diameter on the ground just before the root flayers. 

3. dbh - Refers to diameter at breast height. It is the trunk's diameter before it starts splitting into branches. 

4. mort - Refers to mortality. 

5. fec - Refers to fecundity. Signifies the reproductive nature of the tree. Mortality tends to shrink the populatiob whereas fecundity tends to expand it.
